FAQs

User Payment Services by mRUPEE

1. What is mRUPEE

A. mRUPEE is a Brand Name owned by M/S MMP Mobi Wallet payment Systems Limited (MMPL), a wholly owned subsidiary of Tata Teleservices Ltd. MMPL has obtained authorization license from the Reserve Bank of India for issuing and operating Semi Closed Prepaid Payment Instruments.

 

2. What is mRUPEE payment Service?

A. mRUPEE offers various payment services to its customers like various online & offline Merchant Payment Services across India which includes Telecom/ DTH recharges, Bill payments, Travel Ticketing, Shopping with Deals & Offers & more online as well as designated retailers available in top 25 cities of India

mRUPEE also offers to another mRUPEE Mobile wallet or Domestic Money Transfer to a Bank account.

 

3. How can I use this service?

A. The mRUPEE wallet can be created, loaded and transacted by any of the following three modes

· Using the mRUPEE app available at Google play store for android phones

· Visiting the website www.mrupee.in

· Visiting the authorised retail channel partner

 

4. How can I get information about the mRUPEE payment Service?

A. You may visit our website: www.mrupee.in, Download mRUPEE Application from Google Playstore for Android Smartphone or Call 51111 (Toll free) for Tata Docomo/ Tata Indicom customers and 18004194196 (Toll Free)/18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111 (chargeable) form other operators. You may also visit authorized mRUPEE stores to avail or know more about mRUPEE services.

 

5. I am not a Tata Docomo customer; can I still use the service?

A. Yes, you can use mRUPEE service irrespective of your telecom operator; you just need to have an active mobile connection from any telecom service provider in India.

 

6. Who is eligible to register for mRUPEE payment services?

A. Any Indian resident who is 18 years and above can register for this service.

 

7. Do I need to submit any document for registering for the service?

mRUPEE provides two kinds of service

A. Limited KYC Service: No documents are required to be submitted to use the service

B. Full KYC Service: A valid Proof of Identity and Proof of Address is required to be submitted

The transactions limits in the two services are different

9. Can I register for Limited KYC service first and upgrade to Full KYC service later

A. yes you can upgrade to full KYC service at any point of time by submitting the valid documents (Proof of Identity and proof of Address)

13. Do I get any alert when the payment transaction is completed?

A. Yes, you will get an SMS alert with the amount debited from your mRUPEE wallet after every transaction.

 

14. Do I need to register the Beneficiary for using this service?

A. Yes the beneficiary needs to be registered in the sender’s account. Details such as name, bank account number, IFSC code of the beneficiary are required for registration..

 

15. Do I need to register beneficiary name, every time for any transaction?

A. No, beneficiary registration is one time activity

 

16. How many beneficiaries can I add in an account?

A. Total 8 beneficiaries can be added at any point in time. If you want to add more beneficiaries, kindly delete the existing ones and then add new beneficiaries.

 

17. In how much time receiver will get the money?

A. Depending upon the beneficiary bank the amount can be sent using NEFT or IMPS. In case of IMPS the amount is received within few seconds by the receiver while in case of NEFT, as per the RBI guidelines it will take minimum of 2-3 hours and this would be subject to NEFT timelines.

A.

19. What are the IMPS Transaction timings?

A. IMPS work 24x7 on all days

 

20. To which all banks money can be send using IMPS?

A. Please visit http://www.npci.org.in/bankmember.aspx for updated list IMPS enabled banks

 

21. Will I be informed when my account gets credited or debited?

A. Yes, you will always get a confirmation SMS after every transaction.

 

22. What will happen if I enter the wrong bank account details and money gets transferred to wrong receiver?

A. mRUPEE is not responsible for wrong bank account details entered. Therefore, whether you are transferring money using a mobile application or by retailer’s assistance, the responsibility lies solely with customer.

 

23. Can I reverse payment if I made it to the wrong mobile number?

A. No. Once you make payment, there no way to reverse it. You should ensure that you enter the mobile number correctly to avoid this problem.

 

24. Do I need to pay while registering with “Buy and Pay” service?

A. No, there is no charge requirement for bill payment

 

25. Is there any other fee for making the payments?

A. There are certain services where you will be charged convenience fee. This is intimated to you prior at the time of making payment.

 

26. How do I pay the above charges?

A. The amount will automatically be deducted from your account. The prior intimation will be given to you at the time of making payment through mRUPEE.

 

27. Where will I get details of various fees and charges in case of any changes?

A. The information will be displayed at our website from time to time. Also you will get prior intimation at the time of making payment through mRUPEE account. These details are also available at www.mrupee.in

 

28. In case of any query where I should call?

A. mRUPEE account holders with a Tata Docomo / Tata Indicom connection can call on 51111 to reach customer care. Calls to 51111 will be toll free. Account holders with any other mobile connection can call on 18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111(chargeable) to reach customer care where the charges will be as per account holder's existing mobile tariffs.

 

29. Can I get an account statement?

A. Yes, you can see the transaction history by logging into the account through app or through web. You can also call our Call Center for these details, provide required details and the call center executive will help you with the desired information.

 

30. What should I do if I change my mobile service provider?

A. You can change the Telecom Service Provider by Mobile Number Portability, but since your mobile number remains the same, it does not affect your mRUPEE services and you can continue using them as before.

 

31. What is the validity of my mRUPEE account?

A. Your wallet is always valid, as long as you do a minimum of one transaction in six months.

 

32. How much money can I deposit in one go in my mRUPEE account?

A. If you have a fully functional account (Full KYC as per the RBI Guidelines) you can load a maximum of Rs. 1,00,000 in your mRUPEE account and if you have a Limited KYC account you can load a maximum of Rs 10,000.

 

33. How much money can I spend in one time?

A. It depends upon the type of mRUPEE account you carry. In case of Limited KYC service, the transaction limit is Rs 5,000 per transaction or Rs 10,000 per month. In case of Full KYC service, the transaction limit is Rs 5,000 per transaction or Rs 25,000 per month.

 

34. Do I earn any interest on the amount in my mRUPEE account?

A. No, the amount in your mRUPEE account does not earn any interest.

 

35. How do I withdraw cash from my mRUPEE account?

A. You cannot withdraw cash from your mRUPEE account though you can transfer the amount to your bank account

 

36. How does mRUPEE Payment services work?

A. You can use your mRUPEE account as follows:
Online (Web or App) - By visiting our website www.mrupee.in and clicking on “Buy and Pay” tab. You’ll be redirected to the respective merchants site for further shopping.
Offline (Over the Counter merchants) – By visiting any OTC merchant, just select the product and make payment using your mRUPEE account. This service would be available in select cities in near future.

 

37. Can I pay for someone else's bills?

A. Yes, you can pay for anyone else.

 

38. When does the biller receive my payment?

A. Your account is debited as soon as you initiate a payment request and will be sent to the biller within two days. Every payment made by you carries a unique transaction ID that can be used to track each payment independently.

 

39. Do I have to pay the entire bill amount?

A. This will depend upon the biller. You can make a payment in full or in part depending upon whether the biller provides you with that facility.

 

40. Is there a minimum amount I can pay?

A. No, there is no minimum amount requirement for bill payment.

 

41. Can I make a payment to a biller who is not on your biller list?

A. No. You can only make payments only to participant billers.

 

42. Will I receive a confirmation of the bill paid?

A. Once the bill payment is successful, mRUPEE will send a message to your mobile number. In case you don't receive a confirmation message, please check "transaction history".

 

43. What if I want a physical receipt (for reimbursement etc)?

A. On payment of bill through mRUPEE, you will only get a confirmation on your mobile screen & an SMS from mRUPEE stating his transaction ID. If you want a physical receipt, you will have to contact the respective Utility service provider. Please note that the payment at the biller is updated at least 4 days after the payment being made using mRUPEE.

 

44. I paid the bill through mRUPEE but my service still got disconnected. The biller company is saying that they haven't received the bill payment yet. I want a refund?

A. Kindly raise a complaint with the Call Centre with the following bill payment details - Date on which payment made, mRUPEE mobile number, mRUPEE transaction ID, amount paid, Name of merchant, due date or last date of payment of bill.

 

45. I initiated a payment on my mRUPEE but the status showed "submitted". What should I do?

A. The status shows that the request has been dispatched but not yet processed. This could be due to a network delay. Please wait for a few minutes for the request to be processed. In case the status remains "Submitted" for more than 15 minutes (which is highly unlikely), please check your transaction history by calling at Call Centre.

 

46. My outgoing calls are barred / temporarily disconnected. How do I use my wallet?

A. Unless your connection has been barred for legal and fraud reasons, you should be able to do application based transactions even when your outgoing calls are barred (say due to no balance).

 

47. Is mRUPEE safe and secure?

A. When you sign up for mRUPEE, you will set your secret personal identification number (mPIN). mPIN is required to authorize transactions and get information about your mRUPEE account. Your money is safe in your mRUPEE account, till you decide to use it. Transmission of transactions is done as encrypted content to prevent unauthorized tampering of your transactions. History of transactions is also stored securely.

 

48. What if I lose my mobile phone?

A. Your money on mobile is always safe by your 4 digit mPIN. In case of loss of SIM/phone, please block your mRUPEE account by calling the Customer Care Executive. When you activate your fresh SIM card with old lost number, you can unblock your mRUPEE account by calling Customer Care.

 

49. Can my account get locked? What should I do?

A. Yes, if we detect suspicious activity on your account, we may sometimes lock the account. This is done to protect your money. You will need to call customer support at 18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111(chargeable) from your registered mobile number for resolution.

 

50. What is mPIN?

A. mPIN stands for mobile personal identification number and is prompted for customer authentication for every transaction initiated by you.

 

51. When should I create my mPIN?

A. Your mPIN needs to be created when you activate your account right after your registration.

 

52. What are the mPIN rules that should be fulfilled while creating an mPIN?

A. mPIN should be numeric and 4 digits in length. There should be no spaces between the digits.

 

53. What if I forget my mPIN?

A. Please call Customer Support at 18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111 (chargeable) from your registered mobile number to reset your mPIN. After necessary authentication, the mPIN is reset and you will need to change mPIN immediately.

 

54. What happens if I make wrong mPIN attempts?

A. After three wrong mPIN attempts your account will automatically locked by the system for security reasons. You need to contact Customer Support at 18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111 (chargeable) from your registered mobile number for getting it unlocked. After necessary authentication, the mPIN is reset and you will need to change mPIN immediately.

 

55. How can I reset my mPIN?

A. Go to your mRUPEE mobile application or call on the IVR and go to change mPIN. You will be asked to enter old mPIN and enter and confirm new mPIN, and then submit. It will be changed instantaneously and you can transact using your new mPIN.

 

56. What happens if I lose the mobile phone signal while using mRUPEE for a transaction?

A. If this happens, the payment may or may not have gone through. To confirm, wait for a few seconds, and then check your account balance and activity history. If you are still unsure of the status of your payment, please call Customer Support at 51111 or 18004194196 (Toll Free)/08866651111 (chargeable) from your registered mobile number.

 

57. What do I need to provide if I call the Call Centre regarding a particular transaction?

A. You should have the transaction ID related to that transaction and provide that to the Customer Support representative.

 

58. What do I do if I want to discontinue this service?

A. You can call up the call centre and place a request for cancellation.

 

59. What happens to the money that I have in my mRUPEE when I close this account?

A. Current regulations don't allow withdrawal of cash from this account. You are requested to spend your balance before you submit an application of disconnection.

 

60. Can my mRUPEE account expire?

A. If you don't use your mRUPEE account for a continued six months period, the money would get forfeited. You will be sent intimation before that.
